About Us

At Vegan Life, my passion for plant-based food is at the heart of everything we do. I’m Emma Carter, a lifelong food lover and dedicated vegan, and I started this restaurant to share my love for delicious, sustainable eating. My journey began 7 years ago in my own kitchen, where I experimented with fresh, local ingredients to create meals that nourish both the body and the planet.

Today, Vegan Life is more than just a restaurant; it’s a community. I believe that great food has the power to bring people together, and I want to show that plant-based eating can be exciting, satisfying, and full of flavor. From hearty bowls to indulgent desserts, every dish is made with love and creativity. Whether you're vegan or just curious, I invite you to experience the warmth and taste of Vegan Life, where every bite makes a difference.

At Vegan Life, this journey isn’t just mine—it’s ours. I’m Jake Thompson, and alongside Emma, I pour my heart into making this restaurant a place where great food and great people come together. I wasn’t always vegan, but Emma’s passion for plant-based cooking completely changed the way I see food. Now, I love creating dishes that surprise and inspire, proving that plant-based meals can be just as satisfying, flavorful, and comforting as anything else.

This restaurant isn’t just a business—it’s a reflection of our values, our creativity, and our belief that food should bring people together. I can’t wait for you to be part of our story!
